THE NETHERLANDS

The candy "Drop"

Drop is in essence black liquorice. It's a beloved sweet in the Netherlands. 80% of the population eats liquorice and in a year a Dutch eats about 2 kg of liqourice. If a foreigner is tasting drop,mostly you will see them pulling strange faces in the beginning. There are around 80 different kind of drops: sweet, salt, hard, soft and combined with other taste like wine-gum and fruits.

Kingsday

Kingsday is an annual celebration where the Dutch king Willem-Alexander celebrates his birthday with the Dutch people. It's always held on his birthday itself: the 27th of April.The Dutch celebrate his birthday with lots of music, dancing, flea markets and fun fairs. And the dresscode:orange! It's one of the biggest and most colourful festivities in the Netherlands. And an opportunity to see the King and his family from closeby.

Birth of a child

When a baby is born, the Dutch have 2 rituals. They put a wooden stork in the garden who is carring a baby. Often the name of the baby is also mentioned. And on their work and at home the parents hand out "beschuit met muisjes."
